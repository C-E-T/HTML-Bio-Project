Where to begin?

One of Cody's favorite hobbies is Coding, he loves it so much he has decided to pursue a course of study in coding, specifically networking, and continues to learn new things when he has free time. Cody's interest in coding started at age fourteen when he got bored of the video games he owned and decided he was going to make his own. After doing some research he decided it was way too complicated and set that in the back of his mind for now. The year after he took a coding course at his highschool where they learned Python. He loved the graphics portion of that class and decided to take the grade twelve class the following year. In that class they were taught Java, this is where Cody struggled a bit and it deterred him away from coding, unfortunately a combination of butting heads with the teacher and stress caused disinterest in coding and Cody decided his future was in science instead, but there was still a small voice in the back of his head telling him to pursue coding.

Post Secondary Studies
